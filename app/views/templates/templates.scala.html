Templates in DPost. Rules

  • Templates should be edited in your favorite editor
  • Templates are here on your disk: @templates.path
  • Templates name convention is simple: @templates.nameConvention
  • Templates should be simple and contain limited number of html elements(this is to be compatible with different e-mail clients)
  • Variables has such view in a template: {$varName}
  • Save templates in UTF-8 format (all good editors have that ability)
  • First line of the template will appear in e-mail subject. Second and following lines will appear in e-mail body.

    • Read more about templates and suggested editors in template.pdf
